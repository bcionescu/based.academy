FAQ


Why does this list exist?

If you have a following online, getting hacked is becoming more common. Recently, Sylvan Franklin's YouTube account was closed, due to someone hacking it, and starting a scam live stream. If this happens, what do you do? How do you reach your followers?

This is the first problem that this website aims to solve.

The second problem is a lot more well-known. As algorithms start to recommend more and more AI slop, genuine creators may struggle to be noticed. I hope that this directory will serve as a means for people to reliably find quality content to watch.

Will I be able to review these creators?

Yes, in the very near future. I think I've come up with an interesting way to do this. More information coming soon.

Do you endorse these creators?

Being included on this site does not equate to an endorsement of everything that the content creator has ever done or said. As the list grows, it will become increasingly difficult to keep track of every one of their posts.

If someone is featured on this site, it means that the community has deemed their content interesting, or insightful. The site operator does not take any responsibility for the actions of the people listed on this site.

This directory is for entertainment and educational purposes only.

Is the owner of this directory also included?

Yes. I am Speak, Machine. As you can see, I am included in the list. Not at the top, as that would be cringe, but somewhere near it.

Can I add someone to the directory?

If you wish to add your details, or those of someone that you follow, to this site, there three criteria that the applicant needs to meet.

One. Their content needs to be similar to the people already on the site.

Two. They need to make quality content.

Three. They need to be consistent over a long-ish period of time.

Follower count is irrelevant, as it is very possible to make good content regardless of popularity. If you believe this person qualifies, feel free to send me an email.

I do not wish to be in this directory. Can I be removed?

If your account is listed on this site, and you wish for it to be removed, feel free to send me an email.
